Welcome to COSMIC Hospital

Cosmic Hospital is a multi speciality hospital. We are dedicated to provide world class tertiary healthcare to people in India by fusing the benefits of modern technology with the clinical acumen of the leading specialists in their respective fields. The technology advantage is complemented by the man power excellence providing sophisticated and specialised medical care at affordable cost. We are a perfect blend of technological excellence, complete infrastructure, competent care and heartfelt hospitality - this is how the people, whom we have been fortunate to serve, define the hospital.
